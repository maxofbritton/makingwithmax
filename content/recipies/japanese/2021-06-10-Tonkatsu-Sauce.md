# Tonkatsu Sauce

## Ingredients

- 1 Tbsp ketchup

- 2 1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce

- 1 1/2 tsp oyster sauce (or soy sauce)

- 1 1/8 tsp sugar

## Methodology

1. Combine in small bowl and whisk.
